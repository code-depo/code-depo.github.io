Insertion sort

  • Start from the beginning of the list.
  • In each iteration pick the first element of the unsorted part of the list.
  • Place it at its correct position in the sorted part, shift the elements right.
  • This is a Θ(n2) algorithm.
  • You may think in best case it is a O(n) but even in that case you will have to shift the elements while inserting hence Θ(n2).
